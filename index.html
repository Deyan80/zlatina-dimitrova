About

Zlatina Dimitrova

Zlatina Dimitrova is a House DJ and producer driven by a passion for electronic music since 2006. With a refined musical taste and a deep understanding of groove, she delivers dynamic performances built around powerful rhythms, infectious energy, and a strong connection with the crowd.

Her sound explores the worlds of Tech House, Minimal Deep Tech, and Jackin House, blending driving basslines, hypnotic grooves, and carefully selected sounds into memorable club experiences.
